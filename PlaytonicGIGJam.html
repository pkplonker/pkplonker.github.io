Playtonic & Grads in games jam - Yooka-Laylee inspired project 

Produced for a game jam, as a collaboration between Playtonic and Grads in games. The idea of the jam was to create a Yooka-Laylee inspired piece. 

I was unable to spend any real time on the project due to other commitments, however I was happy to produce the following as a working prototype over a few evenings.

  • Player rigidbody controller with animation
  • Health and stamina system with UI
  • Collectable system with UI with VFX
  • Enemy movement, interaction and damage
