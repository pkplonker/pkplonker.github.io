About Me - Stuart Heath

Hi There!

I'm Stuart, a mature student at Coventry University studying Games technology.

Within this site, you will find a number of projects that I've worked on. Although if you were to browse my Github, you would find a lot more little prototypes as I love nothing more than trying new things out and learning as much as possible.

Although my Games career is so far limited to my University time, I have, and continue to work on, 3rd party projects where possible and taking part in game jams, both solo and within teams.  

Before my games journey, I was an electrical project engineer for automotive manufactuers and suppliers, delivering a number of small, medium and large projects over 10 years.

For the last 5 years, I've been the co-owner and director of a small chocolate company, selling B2B and B2C.

Aside from all that, I enjoy playing games (obviously!), working on my car (A lovely Land Rover 90) and when possible, camping!       

If you've made it this far, thanks! Have some cat pictures (Peanut & Pickle)
